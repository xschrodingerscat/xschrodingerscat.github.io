Solution of Wave Equation with PML using FEM

1 Wave Equation

$$
\begin {equation}
\label{eq1}
\Large
\frac{\partial ^2{u} }{\partial{t^2}} - c^2 \nabla ^2 u = 0
\end {equation}
$$

2 PML Tranformation

2.1 The operator of PML Transformation:

$$
\begin {equation}
\label{eq2}
\Large
\frac {\partial} {\partial x} \longrightarrow \frac{1} {1 + i \omega / \sigma_x} \frac {\partial} {\partial x}
\end {equation}
$$

2.2 Fourier Transformation for Wave Equation

$$
\begin {equation}
\begin {aligned}
\label{eq3}
\Large
\frac{\partial u} {\partial t} = b \nabla \cdot v = b \frac{\partial v_x} {\partial x} + b \frac{\partial v_y} {\partial_y} = -i \omega u \\
\Large
\frac {\partial v_x} {\partial t} = a \frac{\partial u}{\partial x} = - i \omega v_x \\
\Large
\frac {\partial v_y} {\partial t} = a \frac{\partial u}{\partial y} = -i \omega v_y \\
\end {aligned}
\end {equation}
$$

2.3 PML Transformation in Frequency Domain

Introduce a new auxiliary field variable $\psi$.
$$
\begin {equation}
\begin {aligned}
\label{eq4}
\Large
-iw\psi = b \sigma_x \frac{\partial v_y} {\partial y} \\
\Large
b \frac{\partial v_x} {\partial x} + \psi= - i\omega u + \sigma_x u
\end {aligned}
\end {equation}
$$

2.4 PML Transformation in Time Domain

$$
\begin {equation}
\begin {aligned}
\label{eq5}
\Large
\frac {\partial u} {\partial t} = b \nabla \cdot v - \sigma_x u + \psi \\
\Large
\frac {\partial v_x} {\partial t} = a \frac {\partial u}{\partial x} - \sigma_x v_x \\
\Large
\frac {\partial v_y} {\partial t} = a \frac{\partial u}{\partial y} \\
\Large
\frac {\partial \psi} {\partial t} = b \sigma_x \frac{\partial v_y}{\partial y}
\end {aligned}
\end {equation}
$$

3 Time discretization

Rothe’s method

$$
\begin {equation}
\begin {aligned}
\label{eq6}
\Large
\frac {u^n - u^{n-1}} {k} = b \nabla \cdot (\theta v^n + (1 - \theta) v^{n-1}) - \sigma_x (\theta u^n + (1-\theta) u^{n-1}) + (\theta \psi^n + (1-\theta) \psi^{n-1}) && \\
\Large
\frac {v_x^n - v_x^{n-1}} {k} = a \frac {\partial}{\partial x} (\theta u^n + (1-\theta) u^{n-1}) - \sigma_x (\theta v_x^n + (1 - \theta) v_x^{n-1}) && \\
\Large
\frac {v_y^n - v_y^{n-1}} {k} = a \frac{\partial}{\partial y} (\theta u^n +(1-\theta) u^{n-1}) \\
\Large
\frac {\psi^n - \psi^{n-1}} {k} = b \sigma_x \frac {\partial} {\partial y} (\theta v_y^n + (1 - \theta) v_y^{n-1})
\end {aligned}
\end {equation}
$$
